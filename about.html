Our Story

About Home Accessories

A Nigerian home décor brand built on a simple belief: every house deserves to feel like home.

Our Journey

Where It All Began

Home Accessories was founded by Treasure Iferobia out of a love for beautifully arranged spaces and a desire to make that kind of beauty accessible to everyday Nigerian homes. What started as a small personal passion for sourcing unique décor pieces has grown into a trusted online destination for wall art, vases, cushions, artificial flowers, organizers and thoughtful gift items.

Every product in our catalogue is chosen with one question in mind: "Would this make someone's home feel warmer, calmer or more beautiful?" If the answer is yes, it earns a place in our collection.

Our Mission

To make elegant, high-quality home décor affordable and accessible to families and individuals across Nigeria, while offering a smooth, friendly shopping experience from browsing to delivery.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most loved home décor brand — known for quality, creativity and helping people fall in love with their own homes again.
