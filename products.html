Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?+

Simply click the "Order Now" or WhatsApp button on any product, or message us directly at +234 816 774 1107. We'll confirm availability, pricing and delivery details with you right away.

Do you deliver nationwide?+

Yes! We deliver across Nigeria. Delivery times and fees vary depending on your location, and our team will confirm these with you before your order is shipped.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept bank transfers and other convenient payment options. Full payment details are shared once your order is confirmed.

Can I return or exchange a product?+

If an item arrives damaged or different from what was ordered, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos, and we'll arrange a replacement or resolution.

Do you offer custom or bulk orders?+

Absolutely. For bulk orders, events, or custom décor requests, message us on WhatsApp and we'll work with you to find the right pieces and pricing.
